3. Description of Services

3.1 Hashrate Rental

HashForge provides a platform for users to rent SHA-256 mining hashrate directed toward the BCH2 (Bitcoin Cash II) network via Forge Pool. When you place an order:

  • You are renting computational power, not purchasing or acquiring ownership of any mining hardware;
  • Hashrate is delivered through the Braiins Hashpower marketplace infrastructure;
  • Mining rewards are distributed according to your selected mining mode (PPLNS or Solo) and directed to your registered BCH2 address;
  • Your budget is consumed based on actual hashrate delivery at prevailing market rates.

3.2 Mining Modes

PPLNS (Pay Per Last N Shares): You receive proportional mining rewards based on your contributed shares. This mode provides more consistent, predictable payouts.

Solo Mining: You receive the full block reward if your rented hashrate finds a block. This mode carries higher variance—you may receive nothing or significantly more than expected.
